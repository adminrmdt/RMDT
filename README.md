2. RMDT Vesting Contract
https://bscscan.com/address/0x1246d742A308F616eBde31c736Dd3E74580984fb#code

Description: This contract safely locks tokens and releases them over time based on a fixed schedule.
It ensures that team, advisors, partners, or community allocations are released gradually instead of all at once.

Key points for users:
Tokens unlock only after a cliff period ends.
Remaining tokens are released gradually (linear vesting).
Users can claim tokens anytime once they are unlocked.
The schedule is transparent and verifiable on BscScan.

3. RMDT Forwarder Contract (Meta-Transactions):
https://bscscan.com/address/0x8E90FE1556Ee00E66650616506b2EA597366254b#code

This contract allows users to perform actions without paying gas fees by letting a relayer submit transactions on their behalf.
Users simply sign a message, and the Forwarder securely verifies the signature and forwards the request to the target contract.

4 : RMDT Timelock Contract :
https://bscscan.com/address/0x55032dCFb031e0b3C84241fE619d1b2c15571A92#code

This contract adds a mandatory waiting period for any major administrative changes. It ensures that once a change is proposed, users have time to review it before it is executed â€” enhancing transparency and trust.
